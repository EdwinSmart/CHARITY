Our Mission

UVM CHARITY is a charity group based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to helping children in need. Our mission is to provide essential support and resources to improve the lives of underprivileged children in our community.

Fundraising Initiatives

At UVM CHARITY, we provide easy fundraising options to support our cause. By participating in our fundraising initiatives, you can make a significant impact on the lives of the children we serve. Your generosity will help us provide education, healthcare, and other essential resources.

Support Us

We make it easy for you to support our cause. Whether it's through monetary donations, volunteering your time, or spreading awareness about our organization, your support is crucial in helping us create a brighter future for the children we serve. Together, we can make a difference.
